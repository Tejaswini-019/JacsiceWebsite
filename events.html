CO-CURRICULAR AND EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Students are encouraged to enroll themselves in various extra / co – curricular activities. They are encouraged to take part in all the programmes conducted by the college effectively. The chapters of various professional societies organize regular activities such as Seminar, Guest lecture, Quiz, Group Discussion, etc to provide necessary training to meet the new challenges in the changing scenario of technical education and to improve the leadership quality of the students

TECHNICAL FORUMS

ISTE (Indian Society For Technical Education) IETE (Indian Institute of Telecommunication Engineers) IEEE (Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers) CSI (Computer Society Of India) ISTD (Indian Society For Training And Development)

NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME (NSS)

Our college NSS Unit is functioning with the aim to instill social service in the minds of youth. Every year, our volunteers conduct awareness programmes, Free General Medical Camp, Free Veterinary Camp, Leadership training programme and first aid courses in the adopted villages during the special camp arranged by our NSS unit. Our NSS unit is also involved in sapling plantation and in cleaning in & around the college campus. Our students have attended various awareness programme like consumer awareness programme conducted by other colleges. They have also rendered their valuable service in the Pulse Polio Awareness programme organized by Tamil Nadu Government and in eye-camps organized in association with St.Luke’s Eye Hospital, Nazareth. Our NSS unit is also involved in saving the precious human lives by donating blood.

YOUTH RED CROSS

Youth Red Cross, a student wing of Red Cross Society is functioning in our college. The chapter conducts the following activities:

  • Personality & Character Development programme
  • Training programme on Yoga, First Aid, Fire fighting, Awareness on Drug Abuse, Cancer & AIDS, Blood donation, etc…
